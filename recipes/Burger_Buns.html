Burger Buns

These burger buns are easy to make, and they will be perfect for any burger and sandwiches.

Ingredients

  • Bread flour(500 grams)
  • Milk(340 grams)
  • Active dry yeast(6 grams)
  • Salt(10 grams)
  • Butter(50 grams)
  • Sugar(40 grams)
  • Eggs(2 whole eggs and 1 egg yolk)
  • 1 whole egg(For brushing on top)

Equipment

  • Oven
  • Tea towel or plastic wrap to cover the dough
  • Clean bowl

Steps

  1. Take 70 grams of flour and 70 grams of boiling water. Mix them together well into a shaggy mess and let them cool down to room temperature.
  2. Add all the ingredients(including the dough we made earlier) except the butter into a clean bowl.
  3. Mix all the ingredients well and knead for around 3 minutes. Now we can add the butter. It will be sticky and messy but do not get discouraged, it will come together after around 10 more minutes.
  4. After kneading, round the dough into a smooth ball and cover for 1 hour at room temperature.
  5. Take the dough out onto your table, place the smooth side facing the table, and fold the edges of the ball onto itself. Repeat this for about 6-8 times.
  6. Place it back in the bowl and cover for 1 more hour.
  7. Lightly flour the top of the dough and flip it onto your workplace.
  8. Weigh you dough and divide into 6 pieces.
  9. Take one piece and round it into a smooth ball. Repeat with the others.
  10. Now cover them let the dough balls rise for about 1 more hour, or until double in size.
  11. Beat up 1 egg with a splash of water or milk and brush it onto the buns.
  12. Let it dry for 5 minutes and brush them again>
  13. Bake your beautiful burger buns in an oven thats been preheated to 160 degrees celcius, or 320 degrees fahrenheit.

And now you have perfect soft burger buns! Make some delicious homemade burgers with these buns.
