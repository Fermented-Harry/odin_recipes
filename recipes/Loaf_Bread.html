Loaf Bread

A simple loaf of bread that's easy to make, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Strong white bread flour
  • Water
  • Active dry yeast
  • Salt

Equipment

  • Oven
  • Loaf pan
  • Clean bowl
  • Tea towel or plastic wrap(to cover the dough)
  1. Add all the ingredients into a clean bowl.
  2. Mix all the ingredients well and knead for around 8 minutes.
  3. After kneading, round the dough into a smooth ball and cover for 1 hour at room temperature.
  4. Take the dough out onto your table, place the smooth side facing the table, and fold the edges of the ball onto itself. Repeat this for about 6-8 times.
  5. Place it back in the bowl and cover for 1 more hour.
  6. Lightly flour your table and your dough. Place the smooth side down in the table and lightly press into a round rectangle.
  7. Tightly roll the dough into log and pinch the seams so that it's nice and tight.
  8. Place the dough into a loaf pan and cover for 1 more hour. Also preheat your oven to 170 degrees celcius, or 340 degrees fahrenheit.
  9. Bake it in the oven for 40 minutes.

And now your beautiful bread is finished. Make sure to let it cool down for at least an hour before slicing and enjoying.
